Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2025

DeductMate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data entered into the app, including expense records and mileage logs, is stored locally on your device.

We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools. Your data stays with you.

If you back up your device or data using iCloud or similar services, that data is subject to the privacy policies of those services.

For support or questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@nexolve.com
